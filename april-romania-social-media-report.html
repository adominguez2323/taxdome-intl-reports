Key metrics across LinkedIn and Facebook · MoM March vs April 2026
April snapshot: Facebook organic reach grew significantly (+195% MoM to 7,163), while LinkedIn impressions dropped (-83% MoM to 639). Engagement rates declined on both platforms MoM. Landing page traffic from social dropped 48% MoM (225 → 117 clicks), with LinkedIn referrals falling sharply (39 → 8). WIA campaign drove 77 website interactions from total SM in April.
